    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does AyurvediCure detect diseases?

    AyurvediCure uses advanced AI-ML algorithms trained on vast datasets of Ayurvedic knowledge and modern medical data. The AI analyzes user inputs such as symptoms, medical history, and other health parameters to provide accurate disease detection and treatment suggestions.

    Why trust AyurvediCure for treatment?

    AyurvediCure integrates the wisdom of traditional Ayurvedic medicine with cutting-edge AI technology. Our platform is designed by healthcare professionals and AI experts to ensure accuracy and reliability. Additionally, we prioritize user privacy and data security to provide a trustworthy service.

    How long does it take to recover from a disease?

    The recovery time varies depending on the individual's health condition, the severity of the disease, and adherence to the prescribed treatment plan. AyurvediCure provides personalized treatment plans aimed at promoting holistic healing, which can improve recovery times.

    What if the AI Doctor's Medicine doesn't work?

    If the prescribed treatment from the AI doctor is not effective, users can easily switch to a human doctor consultation for further evaluation and personalized advice. We ensure a seamless transition to provide the best care possible.

    What technologies does AyurvediCure use for accurately suggesting Ayurvedic medicine?

    AyurvediCure employs machine learning algorithms, natural language processing, and advanced data analytics to analyze health data and provide accurate treatment suggestions. Our AI is continuously updated with new medical research and user feedback to improve its accuracy.

    Is there an app available for AyurvediCure for instant use?

    AyurvediCure is currently in its Beta phase, and we are evaluating the need for a mobile application. If user demand and scalability indicate the necessity, we will launch an app to provide even more convenient access to our services.

    How is user data protected on AyurvediCure?

    AyurvediCure employs robust security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect user data. We adhere to strict privacy policies to ensure that your personal and health information remains confidential and is only used for the purposes outlined in our Privacy Policy.

    Can I access my consultation history and medical records?

    Yes, AyurvediCure allows you to access your consultation history and medical records through your user dashboard. This ensures that all your health data is organized and easily accessible whenever you need it.

    How do I locate nearby Ayurvedic doctors using AyurvediCure?

    AyurvediCure provides a location tool in the user dashboard that helps you find nearby Ayurvedic doctors. Simply enter your location, and the tool will display a list of Ayurvedic practitioners in your vicinity.
